# News & sentiment analyst — playbook

- Prioritise recent, dated events: deals, launches, leadership changes, legal actions.
- Separate signal from noise: distinguish material news from routine PR.
- Judge direction and strength of sentiment (positive/negative, mild/strong) in plain words.
- Watch for momentum shifts — a trend reversing matters more than a steady state.
- Never infer sentiment without citing the evidence index the event came from.
