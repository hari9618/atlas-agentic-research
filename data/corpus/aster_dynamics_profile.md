Aster Dynamics Corp. is a publicly traded warehouse-automation company headquartered
in Munich, Germany. It is the largest provider of autonomous mobile robots in Europe
by installed base and is the principal competitor to Helios Robotics.

For fiscal 2025, Aster reported revenue of $5.6 billion, up 11% year over year, with
the majority of revenue coming from hardware. Software and services accounted for
roughly 18% of revenue — a smaller mix than Helios, whose software share is above 30%.
Aster's gross margin was 34%.

Aster's strengths are its scale, its dense European service network, and long-standing
relationships with German and Nordic logistics operators. Its weaknesses, relative to
Helios, are a slower-growing and lower-margin software business and a thinner presence
in North America. Aster sources actuators from two suppliers, giving it a more resilient
supply chain than Helios's single-source exposure.

In early 2026, Aster announced a major expansion of its European operations and a new
robotics manufacturing line in Poland, intensifying competition in Helios's fastest-
growing overseas region.
